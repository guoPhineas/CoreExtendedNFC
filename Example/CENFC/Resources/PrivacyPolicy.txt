Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Overview

This app is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. All information stays entirely on your device.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Specifically:

- No analytics or usage tracking
- No crash reporting to external services
- No advertising identifiers
- No network requests of any kind
- No user accounts or registration

NFC Data

This app uses your device's NFC reader to scan contactless smart cards, tags, and electronic travel documents. All scanned data — including card identifiers, memory contents, NDEF records, and passport information — is processed entirely on your device.

Scanned data is never uploaded to any server, cloud service, or third party. No network connection is required or used during or after scanning.

Local Storage

Scan records, NDEF records, and passport records that you choose to save are stored as files in the app's local document directory on your device. These files:

- Remain entirely on your device
- Are accessible only by this app (protected by iOS app sandbox)
- Can be exported or deleted by you at any time
- Are automatically removed when you uninstall the app

Sensitive Information

When reading electronic travel documents (e-passports), the app may process sensitive personal information such as name, nationality, date of birth, and facial photograph as encoded in the document's chip. This information is:

- Processed locally on your device only
- Never transmitted over any network
- Stored only if you explicitly choose to save the record
- Protected by iOS data protection when stored

Encryption

This app uses standard cryptographic algorithms (AES, 3DES, SHA) solely for authenticating with NFC chips and verifying document integrity as required by international standards (ICAO 9303). No encryption is used for network communication, as the app makes no network connections.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. There are no external dependencies that collect or process your data.

Children's Privacy

This app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be included in the app update. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be changed accordingly.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the App Store support link.
